Four-link audit trail

Every disclosed dollar traceable to an individual SAP journal entry — independently verifiable by auditors

This is the audit trail that drops Big 4 DISE testing procedures from 80–200 hours to 20–40 hours. Auditors run every query themselves. No workpapers required.
Select amount to trace
Tracing $2,203,100 employee compensation in SG&A back to source SAP journal entries via four independently verifiable links.
SQL — source transaction drill-down
compensation_sga_drilldown.sql
SELECT bkpf.BELNR AS document_number, bkpf.BUDAT AS posting_date, bkpf.BLART AS document_type, bseg.HKONT AS gl_account, bseg.KOSTL AS cost_center, ROUND(bseg.DMBTR,2) AS amount_usd, m.description AS gl_description FROM `diplomatic75.CORTEX_SAP_CDC.bkpf` bkpf JOIN `diplomatic75.CORTEX_SAP_CDC.bseg` bseg ON bkpf.MANDT=bseg.MANDT AND bkpf.BUKRS=bseg.BUKRS AND bkpf.BELNR=bseg.BELNR AND bkpf.GJAHR=bseg.GJAHR JOIN `diplomatic75.dise_reporting.gl_dise_mapping` m ON bseg.HKONT=m.gl_account WHERE bkpf.GJAHR = '2023' AND m.dise_category = 'compensation' AND m.expense_caption = 'SG&A' AND m.status = 'mapped' ORDER BY bseg.DMBTR DESC
